The 14-page Legal Affairs Request Management Platform v1.0 PDF is the source of product requirements. Core V1: central legal intake; categories LEG-A..LEG-G; authenticated requester intake with category/priority/deadline/description/supporting documents; assignment/reviewer/manager/department approver/admin roles; request statuses New, Under Review, Waiting for More Information, Assigned to Legal Reviewer, Draft Response Prepared, Sent for Internal Approval, Returned for Revision, Approved, Closed, Archived; AI draft-only extraction/risk/checklist/templates/precedents; human legal final decision; audit/RBAC/confidentiality; dashboards and reporting. Current implemented/partially complete: intake/PDF, roles, request detail, AI queue, checklist, auto assignment/routing, manager/department actions, basic dashboard/audit. Major future PDF gaps: strict backend record/document authorization including restricted LEG-E cases; document version/final artifact model; configurable category approval chains; final response/closure/archive/knowledge base; notifications/reminders; fuller reporting/admin configuration. Treat these as staged enhancements, not rushed UI-only changes.
